FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about using TripSplit.

How do I add an expense?

Tap the orange "+" button on your trip dashboard. Enter the amount, a short description, select a category, choose who paid, and pick a split method. Your group's balances update instantly.

How are expenses split?

By default, expenses are split equally among all trip members. You can also assign the full cost to one specific person, or use a custom split if one person is covering a larger share.

How do I invite friends to my trip?

From your trip dashboard, tap "Invite More Friends." You'll get a unique trip code and a shareable link you can send via any messaging app. Your friends can join with a single tap.

Can I be part of multiple trips at the same time?

Absolutely. You can create or join as many trips as you like — a ski weekend, a summer road trip, a bachelorette bash. Each trip has its own separate expense log and balances.

How do I settle up with someone?

Check the "Settlements" card on your trip dashboard. It shows exactly who owes who and how much. When someone pays you back (in person, via Venmo, however you choose), tap their name and hit "Mark as Paid" to record the settlement.

What trip types does TripSplit support?

TripSplit is designed for any group adventure: Spring Break, Bachelor/Bachelorette, Ski Trip, Camping, City Break, or anything else you have in mind. Each trip type comes with a matching vibe in the app.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. All data is stored securely and encrypted in transit and at rest. TripSplit never shares your financial information with third parties and never stores payment credentials of any kind.

What do I do if I have a problem or bug to report?

Email us at support@tripsplit.app and we'll get back to you as quickly as possible. Include your device model and iOS version to help us resolve it faster.
